Horse

A group of horses will not go to sleep at the same time - at least one of them will stay awake to look out for the others.

  • Scientific Name: Equus Caballus
  • Gestation Period: 11-12 months
  • Lifespan: 25-30 years
  • Speed: 40-48 km/h (Galloping)

The horse evolved over 45 to 55 million years from a small multi-toed creature into the large, single-toed animal of today. Humans first domesticated them between 4000 and 3000 BC. They are a remarkably fast and well balanced animal which helps them to escape predators in the wild.
